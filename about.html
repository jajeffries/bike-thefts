When I'm locking my bike up somewhere I often wonder about whether other bikes have been stolen from that location. I noticed that the police published data on this, so I threw together this little tool to help people out.

Based on your browser location it queries the police.go.uk API for crimes near you in the last month, filters them for bike thefts and displays them on a map.

If you have any questions, suggestions or want to say hi, then you can tweet me @jjeffries1
